# Tool Spec: State-by-State Lending Climate Reports

**Tool Number:** 20
**Route:** `/business-loans/[state-slug]` or `/lending-by-state/[state-slug]`
**Priority:** Medium (Local SEO Play)
**Primary Keywords:** "[state] small business loans" (combined est. 50,000+ monthly searches across all states)
**Secondary Keywords:** "[state] business financing," "SBA loans in [state]," "best business loans [state]"

---

## Purpose

Create 50 comprehensive state-specific pages covering the small business lending landscape in each state. Each page targets "[State] business loans" keywords while providing genuinely useful local information.

**Why this is different:**
- 50 high-quality pages = massive SEO footprint
- Local SEO value (state + city mentions)
- Journalists covering local business will cite/link
- State-specific regulations and programs are genuinely useful
- Creates foundation for future city-level pages
- Competitors have generic pages; ours have real data

**Target users:**
- Business owners searching for financing in their state
- Local journalists writing about business
- Small business development centers and advisors
- Researchers and policymakers

---
