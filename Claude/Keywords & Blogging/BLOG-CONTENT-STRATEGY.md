## Interlinking Rules

### Every Article Must Link To:

1. **At least 1 relevant tool** — The most relevant calculator or interactive tool
2. **At least 1 product page** — Relevant loan product (/business-loans/*)
3. **At least 1 glossary term** — Key term defined in glossary
4. **At least 2 other blog articles** — Related content in same or adjacent cluster
5. **CTA to /get-started** — At end of article

### Link Placement Guidelines

```markdown
## Article Structure with Links

### Introduction (100-150 words)
- [Link to pillar page if this is a cluster article]
- [Link to main tool if directly relevant]

### Section 1: Explanation
- [Glossary links for technical terms]
- [Link to related blog article]

### Section 2: How It Works
- [Link to calculator/tool]
- [Link to product page]

### Section 3: Requirements / Process
- [Link to qualification tools (DSCR, SBA Eligibility)]
- [Link to related article]

### Section 4: Pros/Cons or Considerations
- [Link to comparison tool if applicable]
- [Link to Red Flag Detector if relevant]

### Conclusion + CTA
- [Link to Quiz or Get Started]
- [Recap links to main tools mentioned]
```

### Anchor Text Guidelines

| Link Type | Anchor Text Style | Example |
|-----------|------------------|---------|
| Tool links | Action-oriented | "calculate your DSCR", "check your eligibility" |
| Product pages | Descriptive | "business lines of credit", "equipment financing" |
| Glossary | Term itself | "debt service coverage ratio" |
| Other articles | Descriptive phrase | "learn more about SBA requirements" |
| Get Started | CTA | "apply now", "get started", "check your options" |

## SEO Guidelines for Blog Content

### On-Page SEO Checklist

- [ ] Primary keyword in title (H1)
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Primary keyword in at least one H2
- [ ] 2-3 secondary keywords naturally included
- [ ] Meta title: 50-60 characters with keyword
- [ ] Meta description: 150-160 characters with keyword + CTA
- [ ] URL slug: short, keyword-focused
- [ ] Image alt text includes keyword
- [ ] Internal links: minimum 5 per article
- [ ] External link: 1-2 authoritative sources (SBA.gov, Federal Reserve)

| Article Type | Word Count | Sections |

## Content Production Workflow

### Per Article Process

1. **Keyword Research Validation** — Confirm volume and competition
2. **Outline Creation** — H2s, H3s, key points
3. **Internal Link Planning** — Identify 5+ link opportunities
4. **Draft Writing** — 1,500-2,500 words
5. **Internal Link Implementation** — Add all planned links
6. **SEO Optimization** — Meta tags, headers, images
7. **Schema Markup** — FAQ, Article, Breadcrumb
8. **Review & Publish**
9. **Link Back** — Update older articles to link to new content

### Monthly Maintenance

- Audit top 20 articles for broken links
- Update statistics/rates in time-sensitive content
- Add links to new tools as they launch
- Refresh meta descriptions on underperforming pages
