# QuickLenders Keyword Research

Last updated: February 2026

This document organizes keyword opportunities by priority and maps them to specific page types. Focus on long-tail keywords with lower SEO difficulty where we can realistically rank.

---

## Priority Framework

**Tier 1 — Target Now (SEO Difficulty < 40, relevant to products)**
These are winnable keywords that align directly with our loan products, tools, or content strategy. Build pages and optimize existing content for these first.

**Tier 2 — Target Soon (SEO Difficulty 40-60, decent volume)**
Competitive but achievable with good content and backlinks. Target these after Tier 1 pages are ranking.

**Tier 3 — Long-term (SEO Difficulty 60+, high volume)**
Head terms we'll capture as domain authority grows. Don't build pages just for these, but optimize naturally when relevant.

**Tier 4 — Monitor Only**
High difficulty or low relevance. Track but don't prioritize.

## Notes on Keyword Intent

**Informational intent:** Glossary terms, "what is [term]," comparison queries. These build authority and capture early-funnel traffic. Convert to tools or lead form via soft CTAs.

**Navigational intent:** Brand searches ("quick lenders"). Ensure we rank #1 for our own brand. Monitor for competitors bidding on our name.

**Commercial investigation:** Calculator queries, comparison queries, "best [loan type]" queries. High-value traffic actively evaluating options. These users are ready for tools and may convert to leads.

**Transactional intent:** "Get business loan," "apply for business loan," "[amount] business loan." Highest-value traffic, also highest competition. Direct these to the lead form with strong CTAs.

Most of our Tier 1 keywords are commercial investigation or transactional. That's good. We're targeting people ready to act, not just browse.
